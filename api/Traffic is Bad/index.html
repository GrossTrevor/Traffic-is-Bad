Instructions

  • This application provides various information related to car crashes in
    the mainland United States during 2022.
  • Hover over a county to see details regarding the car crashes that
    occurred in that county.
  • Click on a county to zoom and use the legend to differentiate frequency.
  • Use the buttons on the right to change the filter of the data (average
    severity is the default).
  • To change the data structure used to create the graph, flip the switch to
    the left (max heap is the default).
  • The execution time measures the total amount of time taken to build this
    iteration. Notice the differences based on structure.
  • Thank you for using our tool! Safe travels!
